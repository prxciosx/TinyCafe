# Tiny Café ☕✨

A management game with chaotic elements. Survive 7 days as a barista in a café where invisible creatures mess with everything.

Made for [Game Jam Name].

## 🎮 About the game

You are a barista. Your job is simple: take orders, prepare drinks, serve customers. Keep everyone happy.

But nothing is simple.

Tiny invisible creatures live in your café. They change orders. They mess up your drinks. They make customers act weird. Each day, more creatures appear. More chaos. More stress.

Survive 7 days. Unlock your reward. Take a breath.

## 🕹️ Mechanics

**Customers**
- Spawn automatically over time
- Each has a specific order
- Patience meter - ignore them and they leave
- Must be served with correct drink

**Drink preparation**
- Coffee minigame
- Requires player input
- No instant service

**Order system**
- Different drinks for different customers
- Match the order correctly

**Time limits**
- Customers don't wait forever
- Manage your time wisely

**7-day structure**
- Game lasts 7 consecutive days
- Difficulty increases each day
- Survive all 7 to win

**Invisible creatures (coming soon)**
- Interfere with your café
- Alter orders
- Mess up preparation
- Affect customer behavior
- Increase each day
